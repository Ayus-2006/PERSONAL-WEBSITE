Did you know that honey never spoils? Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible. This is because honey has natural antibacterial properties and an extremely low moisture content.

Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood! Two hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps blood to the rest of the body. Their blood is blue because it contains copper-based hemocyanin instead of iron-based hemoglobin like humans.

A group of flamingos is called a "flamboyance." These magnificent birds get their pink color from the beta-carotene in the algae and crustaceans they eat. Baby flamingos are actually born gray and develop their pink coloration as they mature.
